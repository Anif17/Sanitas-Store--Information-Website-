Sanitas


About Us


Sanitas is Asia’s leading health and beauty retailer, currently operating over 7,200 stores – more than 1,500 pharmacies, in 13 Asian and European markets, including Mainland China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Macau, Singapore, Thailand, Malaysia, Philippines, Vietnam, Indonesia, Russia, Turkey and Ukraine.


Sanitas continually sets the standards in the health, wellness and beauty market, providing personalized advice and counseling in health, beauty and personal care on top of its market-leading product range, making customers LOOK GOOD, FEEL GREAT every day. Since 2009, Sanitas has been the No. 1 pharmacy/drugstore brand in Asia, Campaign Asia-Pacific/ Nielsen’s “Asia’s Top 1.000 Brands” Online Study 2018 of over 8.000 respondents across 14 markets in Asia Pacific region. In Europe, Sanitas is also the leading health and beauty retailer in Ukraine.


Our Company


Sanitas were established in 2005. Through a sole and exclusive licensing arrangement with A.S. Sanitas Group, Iskandar Sharif opened the first Sanitas stores in Malaysia which located at Pondok Indah Mall 2, in 2006. Sanitas is a leading health and beauty retailer in Asia and Europe. Currently, Sanitas Malaysia owned 106 stores (as per March 2019) that located across Kuala Lumpur and Johor, Selangor, and Penang. Sanitas Malaysia also expanded its business to e-commerce in order to getn closer with the customers and able to provide non-stop services. This is an effort to actualized Sanitas mission in Malaysia which is to make the customers always “look good and feel great” everyday.


Our Vision/Mission

Vision

Our mission is to provide clinical excellence, outstanding customer service and sound business principles to become the provider and employer of choice in the areas we serve.
Mission

Sanitas mission is to help people around the world to "Look Good, Feel Great, Have Fun". We offer a unique experience to customers through professional advice and expertise, interesting shopping environments and the promise of great value every day.
